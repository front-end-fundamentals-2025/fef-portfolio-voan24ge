Brochure for Aeon Elenith
About the project
I decided to create a brochure for a spa complex, which I named Aeon Elenith. The name was inspired by the concepts of timeless tranquility and serene relaxation—exactly what a spa should embody. The brochure focused on providing clear and inviting information about the spa’s location and contact details. I wanted to create a design that not only conveyed elegance and calmness but also made it easy for potential visitors to connect with the spa. It was a great opportunity to merge creativity with functionality in a meaningful way.
♡ Anna Volosheniuk 2025
