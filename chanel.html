Magazine "The History of Chanel"
About the project
I chose to create a magazine about Chanel’s history. As someone passionate about fashion, I was drawn to Chanel because of its timeless elegance and revolutionary influence on the industry. The brand’s story, from Coco Chanel’s groundbreaking designs to its enduring legacy, felt like the perfect foundation for a visually captivating project. I incorporated stylized pictures and decorative elements to reflect Chanel’s sophistication and attention to detail. This project allowed me to explore my love for fashion while celebrating a brand that continues to inspire creativity and innovation.
♡ Anna Volosheniuk 2025
