Restaurant "SEAVORANTE"

About the project
I created a logo for a seafood restaurant, featuring an oyster as the main symbol. I chose this theme because seafood is deeply connected to Sweden, and oysters represent both elegance and freshness—perfect for a restaurant focused on locally made food. I wanted the logo to feel refined yet welcoming, capturing the essence of the sea while maintaining a modern, stylish look. This project allowed me to explore branding in a way that reflects both tradition and quality, making the final design both meaningful and visually appealing.
♡ Anna Volosheniuk 2025
