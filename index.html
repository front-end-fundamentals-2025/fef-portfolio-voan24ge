About me

Hi, I’m Anna Volosheniuk, a 19-year-old from Ukraine, currently based in Jönköping. I’m a first-year student in the New Media Design program, where I’m exploring the blend of creativity and technology. Although I’ve gained some experience in graphic design, user experience design, and programming during my studies, I’m keen to continue learning and refining my skills in these areas. My goal is to create designs that are both visually appealing and highly functional. If you’d like to connect, please visit the contact page!
