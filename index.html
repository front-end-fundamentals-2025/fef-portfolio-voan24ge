About me

Hi! I’m Anna Volosheniuk, a 19-year-old from Ukraine, now living and studying in Jönköping. I’m in my first year of the New Media Design program, and I truly love the mix of creativity and technology it offers. I already have some experience in graphic design, user experience design, and programming. I’m excited to keep learning and improving. My goal is to turn my ideas into designs that not only look great but are also easy to use.
